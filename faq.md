# 10k Science FAQ

Open any question below to read the answer.

## Basics

<details class="faq-item" markdown="1">
<summary>What is 10k Science?</summary>


[10k Science](https://10k.science/) is Dynamoid's immersive 3D science learning platform for web and VR. It helps learners explore real scientific data through interactive environments, AI-guided instruction, formative assessment, and educator-facing insights.

10k Science is designed for science topics that are hard to teach with text, diagrams, slides, or video alone: microscopic structures, molecular processes, body systems, environmental systems, and mechanisms that require students to connect ideas across scales.


</details>

<details class="faq-item" markdown="1">
<summary>Who makes 10k Science?</summary>


10k Science is made by [Dynamoid](https://dynamoid.com/), an Oakland-based educational software company with a mission to democratize science education through AI and VR technology.

Dynamoid is the company. 10k Science is the flagship product and platform.


</details>

<details class="faq-item" markdown="1">
<summary>Is 10k Science a product, a platform, or an app?</summary>


10k Science is a product and platform. It includes immersive science experiences, the 10k Science Viewer for web and VR, AI Guides, formative assessment tools, the Educator Dashboard, and a growing content library.

People may also describe it as a science VR app, a web-based interactive 3D science platform, or an immersive science learning platform.


</details>

<details class="faq-item" markdown="1">
<summary>What makes 10k Science different from a video or ordinary 3D model viewer?</summary>


10k Science is designed for active exploration and explanation. Students can move through immersive 3D environments, inspect objects, cross levels of scale, hear guidance, answer prompts verbally, and receive feedback.

The goal is not only to show students a model. It is to help them build a mental model of how a scientific system works.


</details>

## Classroom Use

<details class="faq-item" markdown="1">
<summary>Who is 10k Science for?</summary>


10k Science is primarily for science classrooms, schools, districts, higher education, researchers, and science communicators. It is especially useful when learners need a spatial way to understand science that is invisible, abstract, dynamic, or difficult to observe directly.


</details>

<details class="faq-item" markdown="1">
<summary>How can teachers use 10k Science?</summary>


Teachers can use 10k Science for individual exploration, small-group investigation, VR station rotations, whole-class previews, post-experience debriefs, pre-lab or post-lab reasoning, and formative assessment.

The [Educators]({{ site.baseurl }}/content/audiences/educators/) page explains classroom use in more detail.


</details>

<details class="faq-item" markdown="1">
<summary>What classroom problems is 10k Science designed to help with?</summary>


10k Science is designed to help with abstract concepts, microscopic and molecular science, student engagement, explanation-based learning, formative assessment, and giving teachers better visibility into student understanding.

Dynamoid's 2023 educator survey identified recurring challenges such as limited teacher time, student engagement and distraction, funding constraints, individualized feedback, intrinsic motivation, and teaching concepts that are difficult to see directly.


</details>

<details class="faq-item" markdown="1">
<summary>Is 10k Science a replacement for teachers?</summary>


No. 10k Science is designed to support teachers. AI Guides can prompt and respond to students during an experience, while the Educator Dashboard helps teachers see student progress and understanding.

The teacher remains responsible for instruction, classroom context, discussion, feedback, and curriculum decisions.


</details>

## AI Guides And Formative Assessment

<details class="faq-item" markdown="1">
<summary>What are AI Guides?</summary>


AI Guides are instructional agents inside 10k Science experiences. They can audibly orient students, call attention to relevant structures, prompt observation and explanation, listen to spoken responses, and provide personalized real-time feedback.


</details>

<details class="faq-item" markdown="1">
<summary>How does formative assessment work in 10k Science?</summary>


Students respond verbally to prompts while exploring immersive science environments. AI helps score understanding of key concepts based on those responses, and teachers can review student progress and thinking through the Educator Dashboard.

The formative assessment loop is: explore, prompt, explain, assess, respond, and continue.


</details>

<details class="faq-item" markdown="1">
<summary>Is 10k Science a high-stakes testing system?</summary>


No. 10k Science is positioned as a formative assessment tool, not a high-stakes testing system. It is designed to help students receive guidance during learning and help teachers see where students understand concepts or need support.


</details>

<details class="faq-item" markdown="1">
<summary>What does the Educator Dashboard show?</summary>


The Educator Dashboard gives teachers visibility into student progress and understanding. 10k Science materials describe the dashboard as displaying and summarizing students' verbal responses to prompts throughout the experience in real time and helping educators identify where individual students may be having challenges.

See [Viewer, Guides, Dashboard, And Formative Assessment]({{ site.baseurl }}/content/product/10k-science/dashboard-guides-analytics/) for more detail.


</details>

## NGSS And Learning Design

<details class="faq-item" markdown="1">
<summary>Is 10k Science NGSS-aligned?</summary>


10k Science materials state that immersive content is aligned with the [Next Generation Science Standards](https://www.nextgenscience.org/) by the learning science team at the [Lawrence Hall of Science](https://lawrencehallofscience.org/).


</details>

<details class="faq-item" markdown="1">
<summary>How does 10k Science support NGSS learning?</summary>


10k Science supports NGSS-aligned learning by giving students scientific phenomena and models to explore, then prompting them to observe, compare, explain, and connect ideas. This fits well with NGSS science and engineering practices and crosscutting concepts.


</details>

<details class="faq-item" markdown="1">
<summary>Which NGSS crosscutting concepts fit 10k Science?</summary>


10k Science is especially well suited to crosscutting concepts such as patterns, cause and effect, scale, systems and system models, energy and matter, structure and function, and stability and change.

The platform helps students move across scale, trace mechanisms, compare states, and connect structures to what they do.


</details>

## Evidence

<details class="faq-item" markdown="1">
<summary>What evidence supports 10k Science?</summary>


10k Science has shown strong engagement signals and evidence of promise for science learning in pilot and survey materials. Reported signals include a 137-student pilot, 22 of 28 students preferring VR over reading or video, +15% content knowledge gains in Dynamoid pilot studies, 78% student engagement, and an independent Stevens Institute pilot where over 92% of undergraduates said the experience enhanced their learning.

See [Research And Evaluation]({{ site.baseurl }}/content/evidence/research-and-evaluation/) for more detail.


</details>

<details class="faq-item" markdown="1">
<summary>What did educators report about classroom challenges?</summary>


Dynamoid's 2023 educator survey gathered feedback from more than 60 science educators. The report identified challenges including limited teacher time, student engagement and distraction, funding constraints, providing individual feedback, intrinsic motivation, and teaching abstract or non-visible concepts.

The report also found that 74% of surveyed educators identified budget constraints as a major barrier to VR classroom adoption.


</details>
