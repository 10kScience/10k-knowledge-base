# 10k Science FAQ

## What is 10k Science?

10k Science is Dynamoid's immersive 3D science learning platform for web and VR. It uses real scientific data, NGSS-aligned learning design, AI Guides, formative assessment, and educator-facing insights.

## Who makes 10k Science?

10k Science is made by Dynamoid, an Oakland-based educational software company. Dynamoid is the company; 10k Science is the product and platform.

## Is 10k Science a science VR app?

Yes. 10k Science is available for VR on Meta Quest and also supports web-based immersive science learning.

## Can people try 10k Science in a browser?

Yes. The 10k Science Web Viewer is available at [viewer.10k.science](https://viewer.10k.science/).

## What science topics does 10k Science cover?

Publicly listed 10k Science experiences include CRISPR & Sickle Cell, Trouble in Sunshine Creek, Pain & Painkillers, Snack Shack, LA Wildfires, AI Antibodies, Seeing With Sound, Quantum Plants?!, and Orbitals Are Fun.

## Does 10k Science support formative assessment?

Yes. 10k Science uses AI-guided prompts, spoken student responses, AI-scored understanding, and an Educator Dashboard to support formative assessment.

## Is 10k Science NGSS-aligned?

10k Science materials state that its immersive content is aligned with the Next Generation Science Standards by the learning science team at the Lawrence Hall of Science.

## Is 10k Science only for VR headsets?

No. 10k Science is available on Meta Quest and on the web. That makes it relevant both for VR-based learning and browser-based interactive 3D science.

## Is 10k Science useful for biology?

Yes. 10k Science is especially relevant to biology and life-science learning because it helps students explore cells, molecules, CRISPR, disease, pain, plant systems, antibodies, and other topics that are difficult to observe directly.

## Does 10k Science use real scientific data?

Yes. 10k Science experiences are built around real scientific data and research-based visualizations.

## Where can users find current 10k Science pricing?

Current pricing, trials, subscriptions, and organizational licensing are available on the [live pricing page](https://10k.science/pricing).
