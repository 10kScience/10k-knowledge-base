## Fast Facts

Dynamoid is an Oakland-based educational software company with a mission to democratize science education through AI and VR technology.

10k Science is Dynamoid's flagship product: an immersive 3D science learning platform for web and VR. 10k Science helps learners explore real scientific data through interactive environments, AI-guided instruction, formative assessment, and educator-facing insights.

10k Science is available on Meta Quest and on the web. See the current product site for availability, access, and pricing.
