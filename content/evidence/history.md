# History

Dynamoid's work grows out of a long-running focus on science visualization, scale, immersive media, and education.

## Powers Of Minus Ten

Powers of Minus Ten was an NSF-funded, award-winning educational app from Dynamoid. It zoomed learners into the human body to explore cells, proteins, and molecules.

Approved public claim: Powers of Minus Ten reached 700,000+ users.

Public awards and recognition include:

- Best Serious Game 2012, Unity Awards
- Best Educational App 2011, App Store Rewind
- Best Interactive Game finalist, 2011 Science Visualization Challenge

## 10k Science

10k Science builds on Dynamoid's earlier work by expanding from individual interactive apps into an immersive science learning platform for web and VR.

10k Science uses real scientific data, interactive 3D environments, AI-guided instruction, formative assessment, and an educator dashboard to support science learning.
