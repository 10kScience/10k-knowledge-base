# 10k Science Content Library

10k Science offers a growing library of immersive 3D science experiences for VR and web. The content library is built around real scientific data, inquiry-driven learning, and NGSS-aligned educational design.

Access status changes over time. Always refer users to the live 10k Science site for current availability, subscriptions, and pricing.

## Publicly Listed Experiences

### CRISPR & Sickle Cell

Students explore how scientists can treat sickle cell disease using CRISPR technology, zooming into the human body to see cells and molecules.

### Trouble in Sunshine Creek

Students investigate a vineyard affected by invasive insects, plant disease, and microscopic bacteria.

### Pain & Painkillers

Students explore pain transmission and non-opioid pain relief research connected to UC Davis research.

### Snack Shack

Students explore molecules inside familiar foods and drinks through a tutorial-style immersive experience.

### LA Wildfires

An in-development experience about how microscopic PM2.5 molecules enter the body and create health risks from poor air quality before, during, and after the 2025 LA wildfires.

### AI Antibodies

An in-development experience about scientists using AI-generated antibodies and molecular binding to target disease.

### Seeing With Sound

An in-development experience about whales, evolution, echolocation, skulls, and auditory anatomy.

### Quantum Plants?!

A coming-soon experience listed publicly in the 10k Science content library. Keep description high level until the public product page gives final copy.

### Orbitals Are Fun

A coming-soon experience about atomic orbitals and the relationship between visual models of electron distribution and quantum mechanics.

## Scientists And Creators

10k Science gives scientists and educators access to tools for turning real research data into immersive visual science experiences, expanding researchers' ability to communicate their work with new science communication tools.
