# 10k Science Overview

10k Science is Dynamoid's flagship product: an immersive 3D science learning platform for web and VR.

10k Science helps learners explore science through interactive 3D environments built from real scientific data. The platform is designed for topics that are hard to teach with text, diagrams, or video alone, including microscopic structures, molecular processes, systems that change over time, and phenomena that require learners to connect ideas across scales.

## What 10k Science Provides

- Immersive 3D science experiences in VR and on the web
- Real scientific data and research-based visualizations
- NGSS-aligned learning design by the Lawrence Hall of Science
- AI Guides that audibly guide, prompt, and respond to students
- Formative assessment through spoken student responses
- Educator Dashboard insights into student progress and understanding
- A growing content library for science learning
- Creator workflows that give scientists and educators access to tools for turning real data into immersive visual experiences

## Product Positioning

10k Science is for classrooms, institutions, researchers, science communicators, and learners who need a deeper, more spatial way to understand science.

The platform is especially useful when learners need to:

- See structures or processes that are too small, too abstract, or too complex to observe directly
- Move through scientific environments rather than view them passively
- Connect molecular, cellular, organismal, environmental, and human-scale ideas
- Answer prompts and receive guided feedback while exploring
- Give educators insight into what students understand and where they need support

## Availability

10k Science is available on Meta Quest and on the web. Current access, subscriptions, trials, and pricing are listed on the live 10k Science website.
