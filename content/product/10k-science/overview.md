# 10k Science Overview

[10k Science](https://10k.science/) is Dynamoid's flagship product: an immersive 3D science learning platform for web and VR. It helps learners explore real scientific data through interactive environments, AI-guided instruction, formative assessment, and educator-facing insights.

The platform is designed for science topics that are hard to teach with text, diagrams, slides, or video alone: microscopic structures, molecular processes, body systems, environmental systems, scientific mechanisms, and phenomena that require learners to connect ideas across scales.

## Product Pillars

### Immersive 3D Science

10k Science lets learners move through science rather than only look at it. Students can explore 3D environments, select objects, zoom across layers of context, and move between scales such as body, tissue, cell, molecule, and atom.

This is especially useful for topics where spatial understanding matters: cells, proteins, DNA, blood, plant structures, nervous-system signaling, respiratory systems, wildfire smoke, filtration, molecular binding, atomic orbitals, and other systems that are difficult to observe directly.

### Real Scientific Data

10k Science experiences are built around real scientific data, research-based visualizations, and collaborations with scientists and educators. 10k Science materials describe content created using authentic data from researchers at institutions including UC Berkeley, UC Davis, and the University of Washington.

The [content library]({{ site.baseurl }}/content/product/10k-science/content-library/) includes experiences and learning topics across molecular genetics, plant biology, food chemistry, pain signaling, pharmacology, environmental health, wildfire smoke, cellular respiration, air quality, antibodies, echolocation, evolution, and chemistry.

### AI-Guided Learning

AI Guides audibly guide learners through 10k Science environments. They can orient students, call attention to relevant structures, prompt observation and explanation, listen to spoken responses, and provide personalized real-time feedback.

The AI Guide functions as an instructional agent inside the immersive experience. It helps turn exploration into purposeful sensemaking by prompting students to explain what they notice, how a mechanism works, or how events connect across scales.

### Formative Assessment And Educator Insight

10k Science is positioned as a formative assessment tool, not a high-stakes testing system. Students respond verbally to prompts as they explore. AI helps score understanding of key concepts, while the Educator Dashboard gives teachers visibility into student progress and student thinking.

This creates a learning loop: students explore an immersive environment, the AI Guide prompts them to explain, students respond verbally, AI provides feedback and scoring, and teachers use dashboard insights to identify where students understand concepts or need support.

The [Viewer, Guides, Dashboard, And Formative Assessment]({{ site.baseurl }}/content/product/10k-science/dashboard-guides-analytics/) page explains this product workflow in more detail.

### NGSS-Aligned Learning Design

10k Science is designed to support NGSS-aligned science learning. 10k Science materials state that immersive content is aligned with the [Next Generation Science Standards](https://www.nextgenscience.org/) by the learning science team at the [Lawrence Hall of Science](https://lawrencehallofscience.org/).

The platform is well suited to NGSS practices and crosscutting concepts because students can investigate phenomena, explore models, reason about systems, trace cause and effect, connect structure to function, and move across scales.

## What 10k Science Provides

- Immersive 3D science experiences in VR and on the web
- A growing content library built around real scientific data
- Interactive environments for molecular, cellular, organismal, environmental, and human-scale science
- AI Guides that audibly guide, prompt, listen, and respond to students
- Spoken student responses and AI-supported formative assessment
- Educator Dashboard insights into student progress and understanding
- NGSS-aligned learning design by the Lawrence Hall of Science
- Creator workflows and bootcamps that help scientists turn real data into immersive visual experiences
- Classroom implementation options for individual exploration, small groups, VR station rotations, and whole-class debriefs

## Product Positioning

10k Science is for classrooms, schools, districts, higher education institutions, researchers, science communicators, and organizations that need a deeper, more spatial way to understand science.

The platform is especially useful when learners need to:

- See structures or processes that are too small, too abstract, or too complex to observe directly
- Move through scientific environments rather than view them passively
- Connect molecular, cellular, organismal, environmental, and human-scale ideas
- Explain mechanisms, compare states, and reason across systems
- Receive guided feedback while exploring
- Give educators evidence of student thinking and understanding

10k Science also has potential uses beyond classroom instruction, including higher education, scientific and industrial visualization, and medical or healthcare communication. The [Market Applications]({{ site.baseurl }}/content/audiences/market-applications/) page describes those use cases.
