# Viewer, Guides, Dashboard, And Formative Assessment

10k Science combines an immersive learner experience with educator-facing tools for guided instruction and formative assessment.

## Viewer

The 10k Science Viewer lets learners enter interactive 3D science environments in VR and on the web. Students can explore environments, select objects, zoom across layers of context, and interact with scientific content in ways that are difficult to reproduce with static media.

## AI Guides

AI Guides audibly guide students through 10k Science environments. They can inform, instruct, prompt students with questions, and respond to verbal answers in real time.

## Formative Assessment

10k Science is positioned as a formative assessment tool. Students answer prompts verbally as they move through experiences. AI scores student understanding based on their responses and can provide feedback during the learning experience.

## Educator Dashboard

The Educator Dashboard gives teachers visibility into student progress and understanding. It is designed to help educators identify where individual students are having challenges and provide more targeted feedback.

The educator experience emphasizes:

- Real-time insight into student understanding
- Student verbal responses to prompts
- AI-assisted scoring and feedback
- Support for individualized teacher feedback
- Formative assessment, not high-stakes assessment
