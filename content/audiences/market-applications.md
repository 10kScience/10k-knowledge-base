# Market Applications

10k Science's potential comes from a general need: many important scientific ideas are invisible, abstract, dynamic, or difficult to connect across scales. Immersive 3D environments can help learners and audiences move through those ideas rather than only read about them.

## K-12 Science Education

10k Science can help teachers introduce microscopic, molecular, environmental, and systems-level science topics in a way students can see and explore. AI Guides and the Educator Dashboard support formative assessment and individualized feedback.

## Higher Education

College and university instructors can use immersive experiences to support biology, biochemistry, physiology, neuroscience, genetics, chemistry, environmental science, and related courses where spatial understanding matters.

## Research Communication

Scientists can use immersive visual experiences to communicate research to students, funders, collaborators, public audiences, and interdisciplinary teams.

## Museums, Science Centers, And Public Outreach

Interactive science experiences can support exhibits, events, public science programming, outreach campaigns, and informal learning environments where audiences benefit from memorable visual storytelling.

## Curriculum And Instructional Partnerships

10k Science experiences can pair with curriculum design, classroom activities, teacher professional development, and standards-aligned instruction. This is especially useful where educators need content that fits real classroom constraints rather than standalone technology demos.

## Workforce And Professional Learning

The same approach can be useful wherever people need to understand scientific or technical systems that are spatial, microscopic, risky, expensive, or impossible to observe directly.
