# Researchers And Creators

10k Science gives scientists and educators a way to communicate real research through immersive 3D experiences.

10k Science gives researchers and educators access to tools for turning real scientific data into immersive visual experiences for web and VR. Dynamoid works with scientists and learning designers to adapt real research into educational experiences.

## Applications For Researchers

Researchers can use 10k Science-style experiences to:

- Communicate complex scientific systems to broader audiences
- Share data and discoveries in more spatial, memorable ways
- Support public engagement and outreach
- Create educational materials around their research area
- Collaborate with educators and learning designers
