# Educators

10k Science helps teachers make difficult science concepts visible, interactive, and assessable.

The platform is designed for science topics that students often struggle to understand through text or diagrams alone, including microscopic structures, molecular processes, abstract systems, scientific reasoning, and connections across scales.

## Classroom Value

Educators can use 10k Science to:

- Bring real scientific data into immersive learning experiences
- Help students visualize abstract and microscopic science concepts
- Guide students through inquiry-driven science experiences
- Capture verbal student responses during exploration
- Use AI-scored understanding as formative assessment
- Identify where individual students need additional support
- Connect immersive experiences to NGSS-aligned learning design

## Educator Pain Points

Dynamoid's 2023 educator survey found major challenges around limited teacher time, student engagement, funding constraints, individual feedback, and student motivation.

The same report identified budget constraints as the most common barrier to VR adoption, with 74% of surveyed educators naming it as a major challenge. 10k Science emphasizes web access, classroom fit, formative assessment value, and flexible implementation in addition to VR immersion.
