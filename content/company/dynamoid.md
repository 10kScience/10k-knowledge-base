# Dynamoid

Dynamoid is an Oakland-based educational software company with a mission to democratize science education through AI and VR technology.

Dynamoid creates immersive 3D science experiences that spark curiosity and help learners engage with scientific ideas that are difficult to see directly, especially at microscopic and molecular scales.

Dynamoid's flagship product is 10k Science, an immersive 3D science learning platform for web and VR.

## Team

- Laura Lynn Gonzalez, CEO
- Jack Schonbrun, CTO

## Company Description

Dynamoid builds tools and experiences for communicating science through immersive 3D environments. The company's work combines science visualization, learning design, interactive media, real scientific data, and AI-assisted instruction.

Dynamoid is based in Oakland, California, and has a mission of democratizing science education through AI and VR technology.

## Historical Foundation

Dynamoid's earlier work includes Powers of Minus Ten, an NSF-funded, award-winning educational app that zooms learners into the human body to explore cells, proteins, and molecules. Powers of Minus Ten reached 700,000+ users and established Dynamoid's long-running focus on scientific scale, visual explanation, and interactive learning.
