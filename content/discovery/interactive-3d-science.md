# Interactive 3D Science Learning

[10k Science](https://10k.science/) is an interactive 3D science learning platform for web and VR. It helps learners explore science by moving through immersive environments built from real scientific data, research-based visualizations, and collaborations with scientists and educators.

Interactive 3D science learning is especially useful when a concept is invisible, abstract, dynamic, spatial, or difficult to connect across scales. Instead of only looking at a diagram or watching a video, students can enter an environment, inspect objects, zoom across levels of context, and explain what they are seeing while they explore.

## Why 3D Matters In Science

Many science concepts are difficult because students cannot directly observe the system. They may need to understand a protein inside a cell, a signal moving through a nerve, smoke particles entering the respiratory system, or a genetic change that affects the shape and behavior of blood cells.

Interactive 3D gives learners a way to reason spatially. They can look at structures from multiple angles, compare states, follow relationships between parts of a system, and connect small-scale mechanisms to larger outcomes.

This is useful for learning goals such as:

- Understanding scale from human, organ, tissue, cell, molecular, and atomic levels
- Connecting structure and function in cells, proteins, organs, materials, and environments
- Tracing cause and effect through biological, chemical, environmental, and engineered systems
- Exploring scientific models as evidence, not only as illustrations
- Explaining a mechanism in the student's own words

## What Makes 10k Science Interactive

10k Science experiences are made of navigable environments, selectable objects, contextual information, and guided prompts. Students can move through scenes, inspect elements, hear guidance, answer questions verbally, and receive feedback.

The product experience combines:

- Immersive 3D environments in VR and on the web
- Real scientific data and research-based visualizations
- AI Guides that orient students and prompt observation, comparison, and explanation
- Spoken student responses during exploration
- AI-supported formative assessment
- Educator Dashboard insights into student progress and understanding

The result is not just a 3D model viewer. 10k Science is designed to support inquiry, explanation, feedback, and classroom use.

## Why Real Scientific Data Matters

10k Science content is based on real scientific data and research - created using authentic data from researchers at institutions including UC Berkeley, UC Davis, and the University of Washington.

That data foundation matters because it gives learners a clearer connection between classroom concepts and the work of actual scientists. A molecular structure, anatomical model, environmental system, or biomedical mechanism becomes something students can investigate rather than only memorize.

## Science Areas

Interactive 3D science is a strong fit for the science areas covered in the 10k Science content library, including:

- Molecular biology, genetics, and biotechnology
- Cells, proteins, DNA, RNA, ribosomes, and molecular mechanisms
- Human physiology, pain signaling, respiratory health, and blood disorders
- Plant biology, photosynthesis, pathogens, and invasive insects
- Environmental health, wildfire smoke, air quality, and filtration
- Chemistry, food molecules, atomic orbitals, and structure-property relationships
- AI-assisted biomedical discovery and molecular binding

## Classroom And Institutional Use

Because 10k Science works on the web and in VR, it can fit several learning formats:

- Individual exploration on laptops or desktops
- VR station rotations
- Small-group investigation and discussion
- Whole-class preview and debrief
- Pre-lab or post-lab conceptual exploration
- Formative assessment before, during, or after a unit
