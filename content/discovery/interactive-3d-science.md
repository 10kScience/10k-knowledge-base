# Interactive 3D Science Learning

10k Science is an interactive 3D science learning platform for web and VR. It helps learners explore science by moving through immersive environments built from real scientific data.

Interactive 3D science learning is especially useful when a concept is invisible, abstract, dynamic, spatial, or difficult to connect across scales. Instead of only looking at a diagram or watching a video, students can enter an environment, inspect objects, zoom across levels of context, and answer prompts while exploring.

## What Makes 10k Science Interactive

10k Science experiences are made of navigable environments and selectable objects. Students can move through scenes, inspect elements, hear guidance, answer questions verbally, and receive feedback.

This makes 10k Science relevant for:

- molecular biology
- genetics
- physiology
- neuroscience
- environmental science
- chemistry
- microscopic and cellular processes
- science communication and public engagement

## Why Real Scientific Data Matters

10k Science content is based on real scientific data and research. That gives students a clearer connection between classroom concepts and the work of actual scientists.

10k Science is real-data-based, inquiry-driven, and immersive.
