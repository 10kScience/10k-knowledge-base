# VR Biology App For Meta Quest And Web

[10k Science](https://10k.science/) is a VR and web science learning platform with strong relevance for biology education. It lets students explore biological structures, systems, and processes through immersive 3D environments built from real scientific data.

10k Science is publicly available on [Meta Quest](https://www.meta.com/experiences/5616986801647861/) and on the web through the [10k Science Web Viewer](https://viewer.10k.science/). Current access, subscriptions, trials, and organizational licensing are available through the live [10k Science website](https://10k.science/) and [pricing page](https://10k.science/pricing).

## Why Biology Benefits From VR And Interactive 3D

Biology often asks students to connect things they cannot see at the same time: a molecular interaction, a cell process, a tissue response, an organ system, an organism, and an environmental condition. VR and interactive 3D can help students build those connections by making scale, structure, and mechanism easier to inspect.

This is useful for biology learning because students can:

- Move across body, tissue, cell, molecular, and environmental scales
- Compare healthy and disrupted biological states
- Connect molecular structure to function
- Trace cause and effect through body systems, cells, and environments
- Explain biological mechanisms in spoken responses during exploration

## Classroom Use

10k Science is designed to help students understand biology concepts that are hard to observe directly. It pairs immersive exploration with AI-guided prompts, verbal student responses, formative assessment, and educator dashboard insights.

Teachers can use 10k Science for:

- Introduction or review of difficult biology concepts
- Molecular genetics, protein synthesis, and biotechnology units
- Plant biology, photosynthesis, and disease-vector lessons
- Human body systems, health science, and environmental health lessons
- Pre-lab or post-lab conceptual exploration
- Small-group discussion and explanation
- VR station rotations with web-based participation for the rest of the class
- Formative assessment based on spoken student explanations

## NGSS Fit

Biology experiences in 10k Science can support NGSS-aligned learning by helping students investigate models, construct explanations, and connect crosscutting concepts such as scale, structure and function, cause and effect, systems, and stability and change.

Examples include:

- **Scale:** moving from body systems into cells, DNA, proteins, ions, and molecules
- **Structure and function:** connecting the shape and organization of cells, proteins, channels, tissues, and organs to what they do
- **Cause and effect:** tracing how mutation, infection, smoke exposure, or molecular binding changes a biological system
- **Systems:** seeing cells, organisms, environments, and interventions as connected systems
