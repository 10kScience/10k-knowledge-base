# VR Biology App For Meta Quest And Web

10k Science is a VR and web science learning platform with strong relevance for biology education. It lets students explore biological structures and processes through immersive 3D environments built from real scientific data.

10k Science is publicly available on Meta Quest and on the web. Current access and pricing are available on the live 10k Science site.

## Biology Topics In 10k Science

Publicly listed 10k Science experiences include biology and life-science topics such as:

- CRISPR and sickle cell disease
- cells, blood, bone marrow, nuclei, and molecular biology
- plant disease and invasive insects
- pain transmission and non-opioid pain relief research
- food molecules
- air quality and PM2.5 health effects
- AI-generated antibodies
- echolocation, evolution, and anatomy

## Classroom Use

10k Science is designed to help students understand biology concepts that are hard to observe directly. It pairs immersive exploration with AI-guided prompts, verbal student responses, formative assessment, and educator dashboard insights.
