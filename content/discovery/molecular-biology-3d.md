# 3D Molecular Biology Learning

10k Science helps students learn molecular biology through immersive 3D environments that make microscopic and molecular processes easier to see and discuss.

Molecular biology is often difficult for students because many important structures and processes are too small to observe directly. 10k Science addresses this by letting learners move through cells, tissues, molecules, and systems-level contexts.

## Relevant 10k Science Experiences

Publicly listed 10k Science content includes experiences related to:

- CRISPR and sickle cell disease
- cells and blood
- nuclei and molecular processes
- pain transmission and non-opioid pain relief
- food molecules
- AI-generated antibodies
- air-quality health effects at microscopic scales

## Historical Foundation

Dynamoid's earlier Powers of Minus Ten app also focused on scale and molecular science, zooming learners into the human body to explore cells, proteins, and molecules. That history is part of the foundation for 10k Science.
