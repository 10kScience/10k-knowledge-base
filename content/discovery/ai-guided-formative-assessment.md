# AI-Guided Formative Assessment For Science

10k Science uses AI-guided prompts, spoken student responses, AI-scored understanding, and an Educator Dashboard to support formative assessment in science.

This is not positioned as high-stakes testing. The formative focus helps students receive guidance and helps educators see where students understand concepts or need additional support.

## How It Works At A High Level

In 10k Science, AI Guides audibly guide students through immersive science environments. Students respond verbally to prompts as they explore. AI scores student understanding based on those responses, and the Educator Dashboard helps teachers identify where individual students may be having challenges.

## Why This Matters For Teachers

Dynamoid's educator survey identified limited teacher time, student engagement, funding constraints, providing individual feedback, and encouraging student motivation as major challenges. AI-guided formative assessment is one way 10k Science addresses the feedback and visibility problem.
