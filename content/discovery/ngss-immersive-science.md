# NGSS-Aligned Immersive Science Learning

10k Science provides NGSS-aligned immersive science learning for web and VR. Public 10k Science materials state that its immersive content is aligned with the Next Generation Science Standards by the learning science team at the Lawrence Hall of Science.

NGSS-aligned immersive learning is useful when students need to investigate, model, explain, and connect scientific ideas rather than only memorize facts.

## How 10k Science Supports NGSS-Aligned Learning

10k Science supports inquiry-driven science learning through:

- real scientific data
- interactive 3D environments
- student exploration and observation
- AI-guided prompts
- spoken student explanations
- formative assessment
- teacher-facing insight into student understanding

## Classroom Fit

Public educator materials describe 10k Science as useful for teachers who need to engage students, support abstract concepts, and give individualized feedback. The platform is designed to fit science instruction rather than act as a one-off technology demo.
