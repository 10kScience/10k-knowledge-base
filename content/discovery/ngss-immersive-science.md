# NGSS-Aligned Immersive Science Learning

[10k Science](https://10k.science/) provides NGSS-aligned immersive science learning for web and VR. 10k Science materials state that its immersive content is aligned with the [Next Generation Science Standards](https://www.nextgenscience.org/) by the learning science team at the [Lawrence Hall of Science](https://lawrencehallofscience.org/).

NGSS-aligned immersive learning is useful when students need to investigate, model, explain, and connect scientific ideas rather than only memorize facts.

## Why Immersive 3D Fits NGSS

The NGSS emphasize three-dimensional learning: students make sense of phenomena through science and engineering practices, disciplinary core ideas, and crosscutting concepts. 10k Science fits this model because it gives students scientific systems to explore and prompts them to explain what they notice.

Instead of treating a model as a static picture, students can move through a model, inspect structures, compare states, and connect events across scale. That makes immersive 3D especially relevant for science topics where students need to reason about systems, mechanisms, spatial relationships, and evidence.

## How 10k Science Supports NGSS-Aligned Learning

10k Science supports inquiry-driven science learning through:

- Real scientific data and research-based visualizations
- Interactive 3D environments for exploration and observation
- AI-guided prompts that ask students to compare, infer, and explain
- Spoken student explanations during the experience
- AI-supported formative assessment
- Educator Dashboard visibility into student understanding
- Classroom workflows for individual exploration, small groups, VR station rotations, and whole-class debriefs

## Science And Engineering Practices

10k Science experiences can support practices such as:

- **Developing and using models:** students use immersive models to understand cells, molecules, body systems, ecosystems, environmental hazards, and engineered interventions.
- **Constructing explanations:** AI Guides prompt students to explain mechanisms, not just name objects.
- **Analyzing and interpreting data:** experiences are built around real scientific data and research-based visualizations.
- **Engaging in argument from evidence:** teachers can use student responses and dashboard insights to discuss evidence, reasoning, and misconceptions.
- **Design thinking and evaluating solutions:** experiences involving CRISPR, pain relief, antibiotics, filtration, and risk reduction can support discussion of tradeoffs and interventions.

## Crosscutting Concepts

10k Science is especially strong for NGSS crosscutting concepts because students can explore relationships that are hard to show in a single diagram.

### Patterns

Students can observe recurring shapes, behaviors, and relationships in molecules, cells, organisms, and environments.

### Cause And Effect

Students can trace how one event affects another: a mutation changing hemoglobin, a pathogen blocking xylem, smoke particles affecting lungs, or a molecule binding to a receptor.

### Scale, Proportion, And Quantity

Students can move across levels of scale, from body systems to cells, molecules, atoms, and environmental systems.

### Systems And System Models

Experiences are organized around systems such as blood, bone marrow, grapevines, xylem vessels, neurons, respiratory tissues, wildfire smoke exposure, and filtration.

### Energy And Matter

Experiences can support reasoning about photosynthesis, oxygen transport, cellular respiration, molecular interactions, and matter moving through living and environmental systems.

### Structure And Function

Students can connect the shape, arrangement, or location of structures to what they do, from proteins and channels to cells, tissues, organs, and plants.

### Stability And Change

Students can compare healthy and disrupted states, such as healthy and sickled red blood cells, healthy and diseased grapevines, or normal and smoke-affected respiratory systems.

Publicly listed 10k Science experiences can be connected to NGSS-aligned learning in different ways:

## Classroom Fit

Educator materials describe 10k Science as useful for teachers who need to engage students, support abstract concepts, and give individualized feedback. The platform is designed to fit science instruction rather than act as a one-off technology demo.

Because 10k Science works on the web and in VR, educators can use it in different classroom formats, including individual exploration, small-group investigation, VR station rotations, whole-class debriefs, and formative assessment.
